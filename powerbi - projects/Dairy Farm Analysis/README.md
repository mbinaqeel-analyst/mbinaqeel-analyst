# Dairy Farm Performance & Operations Analysis

## Project Overview
This Power BI project analyzes the operational efficiency and production metrics of a dairy farm. It transforms raw agricultural data into actionable insights regarding herd health, milk production yields, and revenue generation.

## Key Features & Insights
The dashboard is structured around key operational pillars:

- **Production Analysis:** Tracks daily and monthly milk yield (liters) and identifies top-performing livestock.  
- **Herd Health & Inventory:** Monitors animal health status, age distribution, and total herd count.  
- **Revenue & Cost Tracking:** Visualizes sales from milk and dairy products against operational expenses (feed, veterinary care, etc.).  
- **Efficiency Metrics:** Analyzes yield per animal and highlights seasonal fluctuations in production.

## Technical Stack
- **Power BI Desktop:** Used for ETL, data modeling, and visualization.  
- **DAX (Data Analysis Expressions):** Custom measures include:  
  - **Yield Comparison:** Month-over-month yield growth  
  - **Health Ratios:** Percentage of herd requiring medical attention  
  - **Total Revenue:** Aggregated sales across multiple dairy product categories  
- **Power Query (Data Transformation):** Cleans raw data, handles nulls in livestock records, and formats dates for analysis.

## Visualizations Included
- **Gauges & KPI Cards:** Track production targets vs actual output  
- **Heatmaps:** Identify peak production times or high-yield farm sectors  
- **Trend Lines:** Monitor livestock growth and milk production cycles  
- **Filters & Slicers:** Interactive filtering by Animal Species, Health Status, and Date Range
